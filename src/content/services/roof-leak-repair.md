## Why do roof leaks show up in the wrong place?

Water almost never drips straight down. It enters through a gap in the roof, runs along the wood decking or a rafter, and falls wherever the path ends. Your ceiling stain can be ten feet from the actual hole.

This is why patching above the stain usually fails. The hole is still open.

We trace the water back to where it got in.

## Where do most roof leaks start?

Most leaks start where something sticks through your roof. Pipe boots, chimneys, skylights, and valleys are the top four. Shingles in the middle of a roof rarely leak on their own. It is the seams and the flashing around them that fail.

Here is what we find most often in Hot Springs.

| Leak source | Why it fails | Rough age |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rubber pipe boot | Sun cracks the rubber | 10–15 years |
| Chimney flashing | Sealant dries and pulls away | 10–20 years |
| Valley | Debris dams water, shingles wear | Varies |
| Skylight | Seal or flashing fails | 10–20 years |
| Missing shingles | Wind tears them off | Any storm |

A cracked pipe boot is the single most common leak we fix.

## How do you find my roof leak?

We start in your attic and look for the water path, then we go up on the roof and check every penetration. We follow the stain uphill to the real entry point. Most leaks show themselves in about 30 minutes.

We photograph what we find so you can see it yourself. You do not have to take our word for it.

Then we give you a written price. No surprises later.

## How much does roof leak repair cost in Hot Springs?

Most leak repairs cost a few hundred dollars once we know the source. Replacing a pipe boot or resealing flashing is quick work. The price climbs if water has been getting in long enough to rot the wood decking underneath.

That is the real reason to call early. A $300 boot today beats a $3,000 decking job next year.

We do not quote leaks over the phone. Every roof is different.
