## Why choose a metal roof in Arkansas?

A metal roof lasts 40 to 70 years and shrugs off the hail and wind that wreck shingles. Arkansas sits at the edge of hail alley. If you plan to stay in your home, metal often costs less over time than buying two shingle roofs.

Here is how the two compare on a typical Hot Springs home.

| What matters | Metal roof | Shingle roof |
| --- | --- | --- |
| How long it lasts | 40–70 years | 15–25 years |
| Upkeep | Occasional inspection | Inspect, patch, replace |
| Hail | Dents, rarely leaks | Loses granules, fails |
| Wind rating | Up to 140 mph | 60–130 mph |
| Summer heat | Reflects sun | Soaks up sun |
| Cost over 50 years | One roof | Two or three roofs |

## What kind of metal roofs do you install?

We install two kinds. Standing seam hides the fasteners under raised seams. Exposed fastener panels screw straight through the metal. Standing seam costs more and lasts longer.

Standing seam is the better pick for most homes. There are no screw holes on the surface, so there is nothing to leak as the roof ages. The panels lock together and move a little when the metal expands in July heat.

Exposed fastener panels cost less. They work well on shops, barns, and porches. The screws have rubber washers that dry out over 15 to 20 years, so plan on having them checked.

## How much does a metal roof cost in Hot Springs?

It depends on four things: the size of your roof, its shape, the panel you pick, and what we find underneath. A simple ranch with one roof line prices out very differently than a big home with valleys and dormers. We inspect your roof for free and give you an exact number in writing.

Here is what moves your price:

- **Size.** More square feet means more panels and more labor.
- **Panel type.** Standing seam costs more than exposed fastener.
- **Roof shape.** Valleys, chimneys, and dormers all need flashing and hand work.
- **What is under it.** If we find rotten decking, we replace it.

You get a real number, in writing, before anything starts. We do not quote roofs over the phone.

## How long does a metal roof take to install?

Most metal roofs take two to five days. Standing seam takes longer than shingles because every panel is cut and locked by hand.

Weather matters. We do not install metal in the rain. If a storm rolls through mid-job, we dry the roof in and come back.
