## Why do gutters matter more than people think?

Your roof sheds thousands of gallons of water a year. Gutters decide where that water goes. Without them, it falls straight down and pools against your foundation.

Wet soil against a foundation is how you get cracks, a damp crawlspace, and repairs that cost more than your roof did.

Gutters are the cheapest insurance on your house.

## How much do new gutters cost in Hot Springs?

It depends on three things: how many feet of gutter your roof line needs, how high the roof is, and whether you want gutter guards. A small single-story house prices out very differently than a large two-story. We measure your home for free and give you a written price.

Here is what moves the number:

- **Linear feet.** More roof edge means more gutter.
- **Height.** Two-story work takes longer and needs more setup.
- **Downspouts.** More corners and long runs need more drops.
- **Guards.** Gutter guards add cost but cut your cleaning.

We measure your house before we quote. No phone guesses.

## What are seamless gutters and why do they cost more?

Seamless gutters are rolled from one long piece of metal right at your house, cut to the exact length of each run. No joints along the run means almost nothing to leak. Store-bought sectional gutters join every ten feet, and each joint eventually drips.

Here is the difference.

| Feature | Seamless | Sectional |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Joints per run | None | Every ~10 feet |
| Leak points | End caps and corners only | Every joint |
| Typical life | 20+ years | 10–15 years |
| Installed by | A crew with a forming machine | Anyone |

## What are the signs my gutters are failing?

Watch for water spilling over the front edge in rain, sagging sections, peeling paint on the fascia, and dirt splashed up on your siding. Puddles right against your foundation after a storm are the clearest sign. All of these mean water is not getting where it should.

Rust streaks and separated corners are the easy ones to spot.

If your fascia board is soft, water has been getting in for a while. That is a repair, not a cleaning.

## Do gutter guards work around Hot Springs?

They help a lot here. Oaks and pines are everywhere around Hot Springs and the Ouachita Mountains, and leaves are what clog gutters. Guards keep the big stuff out and cut your cleaning from four times a year to maybe once.

They do not eliminate cleaning. Fine grit and pine needles still work through.

We will tell you honestly if your house has enough tree cover to justify them.
