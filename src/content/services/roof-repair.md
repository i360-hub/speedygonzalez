## Why fix a small roof problem now?

A small roof problem never stays small. One missing shingle lets water reach the wood decking underneath. That wood rots quietly for months. By the time you see a ceiling stain, the repair costs several times more.

Arkansas weather speeds this up. We get severe thunderstorms, spring hail, and long stretches of heat.

Catching it early is the whole game.

## How much does roof repair cost in Hot Springs?

It depends on what is wrong and how far the damage spread. Replacing a few shingles, resealing a pipe boot, or fixing one section of flashing are all small jobs. The price climbs once water has rotted the decking underneath. We inspect your roof for free and give you a written price.

Here is what typical repairs involve.

| Problem | What we do | Job size |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Missing shingles | Match and replace | Small |
| Leaking pipe boot | Replace the boot | Small |
| Bad valley flashing | Pull back shingles, reflash | Medium |
| Rotten decking | Cut out wood, rebuild, reroof area | Medium |
| Storm-torn section | Rebuild the whole section | Large |

We do not quote roofs over the phone. We go up and look.

## What are the signs my roof needs repair?

Look for water stains on your ceiling, missing or curling shingles, and granules collecting in your gutters. Damp spots in your attic and daylight through the roof boards are urgent. Any one of these means you should get up there, or call someone who will.

You do not need to climb a ladder yourself. Most of what we find is visible from the ground with a good eye and binoculars.

Our inspection takes about 30 minutes and costs nothing.

## Should I repair or replace my roof?

Repair when the trouble is in one spot and the rest of the roof is sound. Replace when the wear covers the whole roof or the roof is past 20 years old. Patching a worn-out roof just moves the leak somewhere else.

We will tell you which one you have. If a small repair buys you five more years, we do the repair.

If your roof is done, look at [shingle roofing](/services/shingle-roofing) or [metal roofing](/services/metal-roofing). Metal lasts 40 to 70 years and handles hail better.

## What if a storm caused the damage?

Then your insurance may pay for it. Wind, hail, and fallen limbs are covered on most policies. Age and normal wear are not.
