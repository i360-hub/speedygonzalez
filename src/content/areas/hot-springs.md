## Do you serve all of Hot Springs?

Yes. Hot Springs is our home. Our shop sits at 207 Albert Pike Rd, right in the middle of town. We roof homes from downtown out to Lake Hamilton, Lake Ouachita, and everywhere between.

Being local matters here. We know which streets are steep. We know which lots need a smaller truck. You are not waiting on a crew from another state.

## Why do Hot Springs roofs take such a beating?

Trees and hills. Hot Springs sits in the Ouachita Mountains, and tall oaks and pines shade most older neighborhoods. That shade holds moisture on the shingles. Limbs scrape. Leaves pack the valleys and gutters.

Then add Arkansas weather. Spring hail and summer heat both punish a roof. Here is what we see most across town.

| Part of town | What roofs look like | What we fix most |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Downtown and Bathhouse Row | Older, steep, tight lots | Leaks, flashing, decking |
| Lake Hamilton | Mixed ages, big trees | Storm damage, moss, gutters |
| Lake Ouachita side | Rural, longer drives | Metal roofs, full replacement |
| Oaklawn area | Ranch homes, easier access | Hail damage, shingle replacement |
