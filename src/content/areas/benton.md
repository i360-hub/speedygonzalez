## Why do newer Benton roofs still fail?

Hail. A roof does not have to be old to be finished. Many homes in Benton's newer subdivisions went up with basic builder shingles, and those give up quickly under Arkansas hail.

The roof can look fine from your driveway and still leak. Hail knocks the granules off. Without granules, the shingle bakes and cracks.
