## Why do Pine Bluff roofs need extra attention?

Age. Pine Bluff has a lot of older housing stock, and older houses have older roofs. Many have layers of roofing stacked on top of each other.

Old layers hide problems. Under them you often find rotten decking, worn flashing, and vents that quit working years ago. You cannot see any of that from the ground.

## Is an old house worth reroofing?

Often yes. An older Pine Bluff home can have solid bones under a bad roof. The roof is what protects the rest of the house.

Once water gets in, it goes after the decking, then the framing, then your ceilings. Fixing the roof is almost always cheaper than fixing what water ruined.
