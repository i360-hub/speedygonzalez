## Does all that Malvern brick affect my roof?

At the edges, yes. Malvern is the Brick Capital of the World, and brick homes are everywhere here. The brick does not hurt the roof. The joints where brick meets roofing do.

Chimneys, dormers, and side walls all need flashing cut and sealed into the brick properly. Done wrong, water runs behind it for years before you see a stain inside.

| Where brick meets roof | What goes wrong | What we do |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Chimney | Old caulk, no step flashing | Cut in new flashing |
| Side walls | Sealant instead of metal | Proper step flashing |
| Dormers | Water runs behind brick | Rebuild the joint |
| Chimney cap | Cracks, open joints | Seal or replace cap |
