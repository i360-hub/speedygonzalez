## Do you roof inside Hot Springs Village?

Yes, every week. The Village is one of the biggest gated communities in the country, and it sits right next door to our Hot Springs shop. We work on both sides of the East Gate and the West Gate.

We know the drill at the gates. We know the roads wind. Our crews plan for it.

## Do I need POA approval to replace my roof?

Usually yes. The POA reviews architectural changes in the Village, and your roof is one of them. If you change the color or the material, expect to get approval first.

We are used to this. We can tell you what the POA will want to see and help you get your paperwork together before we order material.

| What you want to do | POA approval | How hard is it |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Same shingle, same color | Usually simple | Easy |
| New shingle color | Approval needed | Straightforward |
| Shingles to metal | Approval needed | Plan ahead |
| Emergency leak repair | Fix first, ask after | We tarp same day |

Always confirm current rules with the POA. They set the standard, not us.

## Why do Village roofs wear out under the trees?

Because the woods stayed. The Village was built into the forest, so pines and oaks lean over most homes. Needles fill the valleys. Leaves pack the gutters.

That debris holds water against the roof. Wet shingles grow moss and rot the deck underneath. Village roofs need a look more often than roofs in open subdivisions.
